BIW Fixture Design

BIW fixtures play a critical role in the automotive manufacturing process by ensuring that the body components of a vehicle are accurately positioned and held in place during assembly. Our BIW fixture designs provide robust solutions that are essential for the assembly line, enhancing the overall production efficiency and maintaining high precision during the manufacturing process.

    Key Feature

  • High Precision Fixture Designs: Using advanced CAD/CAM software, we create fixtures with tight tolerances that ensure the accurate alignment of automotive body parts during assembly.
  • Optimized Production Efficiency: Our designs are aimed at improving the throughput of the production line by minimizing downtime and ensuring easy handling of parts during the assembly process.
  • Compliance with Automotive Standards: All fixture designs adhere to industry standards, ensuring quality control, consistency, and safety during manufacturing.

Our design process considers factors like part geometry, material properties, and assembly line constraints. The result is a fixture that guarantees precise positioning while being easy to handle and set up. We also focus on designing fixtures that are adaptable to accommodate various body styles and future upgrades.

Simulation

Simulation is at the core of modern manufacturing, enabling businesses to test processes virtually before physical implementation. By using tools like Process Simulate and Dalmia, we help our clients achieve optimal manufacturing processes, robotic programming, and ergonomics analysis, saving time and reducing costly mistakes.

    Services Include:

  • Process Validation & Optimization: We simulate the entire production process to identify bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and areas for improvement, ensuring that your manufacturing process is fully optimized.
  • Robotic Programming Simulation: We utilize advanced robotic programming simulations to verify and validate robot movement and programming before actual deployment on the production floor.
  • Ergonomic Analysis: By simulating the workspace and the assembly process, we can optimize ergonomics, reducing physical strain on workers and improving productivity.

Our simulation tools can model entire production lines, integrating robotic automation, human labor, and logistics. This virtual testing leads to greater accuracy, fewer errors, and the ability to make real-time adjustments during the planning phase.

SPM

Special Purpose Machines (SPMs) are custom-built machines designed to handle specific manufacturing tasks that standard machines cannot perform. These machines help automate complex, specialized processes, reducing manual labor and improving precision.

    Key Benefits:

  • Custom-Built Solutions: We design and build SPMs tailored to meet unique production needs. Whether it’s an automated machine for welding, inspection, or assembly, we ensure that each solution is specific to your requirements.
  • Enhanced Productivity & Accuracy: Our SPMs increase the speed and accuracy of operations, reducing the chances of defects and improving overall efficiency.
  • Cost-Effective Automation: By automating specialized processes, businesses can reduce labor costs and improve throughput, making the entire production process more cost-effective.

We start by analyzing the client’s production requirements and then design machines that provide solutions for the toughest manufacturing challenges. Each machine is rigorously tested to meet performance standards before it’s integrated into the production line.
